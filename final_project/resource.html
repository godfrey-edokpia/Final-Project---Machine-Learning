Scoring Data

Any average temperatures between 70 degrees t0 80 degrees without rain and snow is ideal to host an outside event. We used Python to score our average temperatures. A score of 1 = Bad Day and a score of 2 = Good Day.

As a result, when the machine learning part ran, it correctly picked a good day 86% of the time and correctly picked a bad day 44% of the time.
